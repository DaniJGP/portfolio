About me

Welcome! I'm Daniela, a full-stack web developer with a translation background, always eager to learn new technologies and expand my skillset.

I'm an optimist at heart, exploring ways to build applications that are not only technically sound, but also broadly accessible. My goal is to contribute to creating a more inclusive digital space that breaks down barriers and brings people closer together, either as a solo developer or working in a team that shares these beliefs.

I'm currently learning the ins and outs of CSS animations, accessibility and the use of SVG sprites for icons; you'll be able to read more about this in my blog, coming soon!

When not coding, you'd probably find me immersing myself in Japanese material to prepare for the JLPT or learning and playing riffs on my electric guitar. I'm also a lover of strategy games and and avid Formula 1 fanatic.
